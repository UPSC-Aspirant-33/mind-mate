Things to Keep in Mind While Preparing

(Free advice keatta kettukko 😁 kekkatti po 😏)

1
Limit distractions
Don’t rely on “I’ll control myself” — it rarely works.

Keep a fixed time for phone use (30–45 minutes a day)
Log out or remove apps like Instagram and reels
Keep your phone in another room while studying

Rule: If it’s near you, you’ll use it.
2
Get comfortable being alone
This is an important part of the journey.

Sit for 1–2 hours daily without phone or music
Avoid constant chatting during study time

At first it may feel boring. Over time, it becomes normal.

That’s when real focus begins.
3
Maintain balance
Balance doesn’t mean equal time for study and relaxation.

Study is the priority
Take small breaks like walking, music, or talking to a few people

Rule: Breaks should refresh you, not distract you.
4
Discipline over motivation
Motivation doesn’t last long.

Study at the same time every day
Even on difficult days, do at least one hour

That minimum effort is what keeps consistency alive.
5
Take care of mental health
Don’t ignore signs like overthinking, burnout, or self-doubt.

Write your thoughts for a few minutes
Talk to someone you trust
Take one light day each week

This is not weakness — it is necessary care.
6
Consistency
Consistency is not studying long hours once in a while.

Study 2–4 hours daily without long breaks

Don’t aim for big efforts — aim for something repeatable every day.

Consistency is what works in the long run.
